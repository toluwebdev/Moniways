About Us

Moniways Technologies Limited (RC 1775946) is a digital financial service company that creates an enabling platform for our clientele by providing top-notch payment solutions through seamless technology, commitment to excellence, and the passion to serve our customers better. We offer a suite of financial technology solutions to individuals, businesses and government agencies.

These solutions include cashout, deposit, savings, withdrawal, bill payments, collections, remittances, cross-border payments and API services. Our products are designed to be easy to use, secure, and accessible to those who have traditionally been excluded from the financial system. By creating an enabling platform, our customers can do more and be more. The business commenced in January 2021 with 532 users which later grew to 23982 users spread across Nigeria, we also have business presence in Togo, Liberia, Sierra Leone Cyprus and Turkey.
